# Voice

## Tone

The brand should sound precise, institutional, and direct.

Use language that emphasizes:

- compliance intelligence
- explainable evidence
- screening, reasoning, reporting
- institutional-grade controls
- audit trails
- regulator-ready outputs
- operational speed

## Copy Register

Headlines:

- short
- declarative
- product/market specific
- no generic SaaS optimism

Body:

- specific and useful
- grounded in workflows and outcomes
- acceptable to compliance, legal, risk, and institutional buyers

Labels:

- mono uppercase
- short nouns or noun phrases
- operational categories

## Naming

Core terms:

- the brand
- Intelligence Layer
- digital asset compliance
- SR3 stack
- Strix AI Agent
- Screen, Research, Reasoning, Report

## Avoid

- vague AI claims
- playful crypto slang
- consumer-app language
- fluffy transformation claims
- "seamless" unless describing a concrete integration behavior

# Nothing Design System — Logo

## Wordmark

Use a geometric sans wordmark treatment with restrained weight and tight, deliberate spacing. Keep the wordmark black, white, or text-primary; red is reserved for signal marks.

## Monogram

If a monogram exists, make it circular or dot-matrix compatible. It should work at favicon size and as a UI control mark.

## Lockups

| Lockup | Use | Rule |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Horizontal | Product header, marketing nav | Wordmark plus optional mono descriptor |
| Stacked | Presentation or manual cover | Large clear space, no decoration |
| Mark-only | App icon, favicon, compact nav | Must remain legible at 16px |

## Misuse

- Do not add gradients, shadows, bevels, or glow.
- Do not place the mark inside a rounded card unless the product surface requires it.
- Do not use red for the full wordmark.
